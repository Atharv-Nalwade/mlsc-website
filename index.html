Who are we?

Established in June 2023, the Microsoft Learn Students' Club (MLSC) is a dynamic hub for fostering technological growth through Microsoft technologies. We cultivate coding skills, critical thinking, and logic in a supportive environment. Our mission is to drive development via hands-on projects, international collaborations, and innovative seminars, all centered around Microsoft solutions.
